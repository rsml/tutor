Series Grouping Rules

  • Series groups render first, sorted by the series' earliest book's creation date (or by sort preference). Within a series, books are ordered by seriesOrder.
  • "Library" section contains ungrouped books, sorted by the current sort preference.
  • When filtering by a specific series, only that series' books show (no "Library" section). Series header still visible for context.
  • "Book N" label on series cards shows the seriesOrder — e.g., "Book 2" for seriesOrder: 2.
  • Series with 1 book — still groups, shows header. This is intentional — the user may be building the series incrementally.
  • List view — series header is a collapsible row. Column headers are sortable. Tags shown as inline chips.
